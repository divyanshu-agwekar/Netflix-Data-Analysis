# Netflix Data Analysis

## 📊 Objective
To analyze Netflix dataset and extract meaningful insights about content trends.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib

## 📈 Analysis Performed
- Movies vs TV Shows comparison
- Content released over years
- Top 10 content producing countries
- Genre analysis
- Recent trends (after 2015)

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Most Netflix content has been released after 2015
- Movies are more in number compared to TV Shows
- USA produces the highest amount of content
- Drama is among the most popular genres
- Significant growth in content production in recent years

## ✅ Conclusion
Netflix has significantly expanded its content library in recent years, with a strong dominance of movies and major contribution from the United States.# Netflix-Data-Analysis
